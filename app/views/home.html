About

During my employment at an online sheet music retailer, I rediscovered my interest in finding solutions to problems. It wasn't long before I decided to pursue an education in web development. In June of 2015 I graduated as part of the 17th cohort of General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program.

Since then, while living in San Francisco and Napa Valley, I have refined my Javascript skills working with various libraries and frameworks like jQuery, Bootstrap, Angular, Vue and React. I have implemented responsive design building apps that use various relational and non-relational databases as well as web services by writing clean, easily maintainable, composable code that is easily adaptable to changing requirements. I have experience working in both start-ups like Konversai and large corporations like Levi Strauss, and I understand the dynamics and responsibilities of each.
